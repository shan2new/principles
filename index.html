Code of Honor

Twelve timeless principles for samurai spirits and noble knights

Principles I: 1–6

  • Fairness: Impartial treatment and equal opportunity.
  • Equity: Tailored support to achieve true fairness.
  • Justice: Giving each their due under consistent standards.
  • Integrity: Unwavering adherence to moral principles.
  • Service: Acting compassionately to benefit others.
  • Excellence: Consistently doing one’s best work.

Principles II: 7–12

  • Growth: Ongoing development of one’s capabilities.
  • Courage: Facing fear and adversity with conviction.
  • Humility: Maintaining an accurate, modest self‑view.
  • Industry: Diligent, focused effort toward useful work.
  • Temperance: Self‑restraint and moderation in actions.
  • Modesty: Letting character speak without ostentation.
